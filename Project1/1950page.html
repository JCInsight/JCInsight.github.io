Paul Anka
Before the rise of pop and hiphop, there was a classic rock and roll. This is not the rock and roll that is very well known today; this is the fast-paced version that drew inspiration from all genres of music. Paul Anka was a music artist of this genre, and I was an avid follower of his music. Just remembering how the music used to make me (and others) feel, especially when it took all the great parts of many other genres and blended them into its own original type of music.
